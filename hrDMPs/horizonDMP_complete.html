1. Data summary

The aim of this pseudo project is to analyze the relation between the global temperature change and the number of children born alive in Vienna. The experiment is conducted, by means of visual data science (exploring the data by plotting it in an appropriate manner).

Dataset - mergedData: Internally generated dataset, that was merge from two external datasets.

The distribution mergedData of the dataset is provided in the format csv and is 362bytes large.
